Who It's For

Whatever your level, there's a path forward

Three distinct tracks. One coach who's been there at every level.

Beginners

Never touched a chess board. We start from zero — the pieces, the rules, the mindset. You'll play your first real game within two sessions.

This is for you if…

  • You've always been curious about chess but never started
  • You want to learn properly, not from YouTube clips
  • Your child needs a patient coach who starts from the very beginning

Improvers

You know how to play but want to compete. We plug the gaps, build your tactical eye, and get you ready for tournaments.

This is for you if…

  • You've played casually but feel stuck at the same level
  • You want to enter your first competition but don't know how to prepare
  • You're losing games and can't figure out why

Competitors

You're already competing and want to start winning. We analyse your game, sharpen your edge, and give you the strategies used at UAE championship level.

This is for you if…

  • You've competed but keep finishing mid-table
  • You want tournament-tested strategies, not generic drills
  • You're ready to take chess seriously

The DeepGame Difference

Every student gets a coaching plan that's theirs alone

Playing Style Identification

Within your first month of coaching, we map exactly how you think and play. Aggressive, positional, tactical — we find your natural style and build on it.

Strength-First Training

Most coaches teach generic chess. We find what you're already good at and make it lethal, so you improve faster than any generic curriculum can offer.

Performance Tracking

Every session is logged. Every game reviewed. You'll always know exactly where you've grown and what's next.
